## Gurdjieff Octave Context

The octave follows the Law of Seven:
- **Do (1) → Re (2) → Mi (3):** First triad (shock, intend, feel)
- **Mi → Fa (4):** The "deviation" or catalyst needed (fall)
- **Fa → Sol (5) → La (6):** Second triad (think, listen, return) 
- **La → Si (7):** Preparation for completion (sing)
- **Si → Do (8):** Return to the One (dance, sit, be)
