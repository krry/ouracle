## Poetics

The Magician starts at the threshold, 
where roots meet sky.
Here, like a shrub, they sit. 
Here we must only be.
The point is stillness. 
To smell and be smelled.
To level up and out and back in.
An entity of earth ready to begin,
the Magician finds ground all around
pragmatism abounds
like the Earth’s support underneath.
