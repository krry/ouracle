## Poetics

‘Round the horn, old bull. Reboot!

Zero out the register. Balance the books.

Catch the pendulum. Let it hang limp.

Scream that silent, original note into the vengeful pillow over your face.

Forget the person you thought you were and remember who you always will have been.

What a reboot, when the Earth’s magnetic field flipped polarity!

Butter up princess, and through the cosmic sphincter with you.

Nobody knows what may emerge on the otherside, but it won’t be no body.
