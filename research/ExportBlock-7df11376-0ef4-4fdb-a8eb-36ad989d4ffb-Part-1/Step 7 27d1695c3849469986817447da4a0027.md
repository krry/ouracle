## Poetics

Universal serial blunderbuss

cloaca spouting and gulping

fountains and rainbow falls

thunder from the blinding sun

joy: the shock of success

abundant release breeds agape

surrender allows for balance

the crown sprouts enthusiasm

a perfect, universal melody

lasting justice and harmony
