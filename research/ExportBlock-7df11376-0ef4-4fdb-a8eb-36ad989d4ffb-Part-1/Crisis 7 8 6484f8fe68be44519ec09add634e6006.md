## Poetics

You are being asked to love the bane of your existence, the stick in your mud, the object of your affliction, the alien in the bedroom. You are being forced to let go, ripped away from the dear downy nest you have woven of stories and sense experiences. The otherside is obscured. An acrid cloud of hate and contemptuous trepidation squats double parked on your brows.

It's darkest before dawn, they say.

The high contrast inflection point, when the pendulum reaches its extreme.

Zenith or nadir? Down becomes up.

Try as you might the night closes in. 

In the belly of the whale, you may as well be food.

Bury your head and the rest will follow.

Quicksand in the oil field, leave your boots or you'll end up tarred and feathered.

Fix! Just kidding, you can't.

These compounding crises will remind you like a thousand lead balloons on strings with fishhooks in your back.

Laying down, zeroing out, you can see the firmament on which the world is mounted, and of which, as it turns out, we are made.

The only way forward is through and vice versa.
