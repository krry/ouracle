**Commit Message:** Can the Contacts app sort by creation date

## Prompt

can the Contacts app sort by creation date?

## Summary

No — the stock Contacts app only sorts by first name or last name. There's no "recently added" sort or filter anywhere in the UI.

Which is interesting, because the data is clearly there in the underlying AddressBook database (the app itself must have access to its own store). Apple just chose not to surface it in the UI either.

"Recently Added" is a standard feature in Photos, Music, and other first-party apps — but Contacts has never gotten it. It's one of those long-neglected corners of iOS.
